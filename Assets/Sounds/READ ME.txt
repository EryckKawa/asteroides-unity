SE - Free 8-bit SFX resource pack?


This is a package of sound resources made by me, and made available for free.

The intention is just to help those looking for fast and free sound effects to use in your project, feel free to use this package in any project, be it commercial or non-commercial.

Contains the following effects

Cancel or Gun - 7 Sounds
Explosion - 12 Sounds
Get-item - 11 Sounds
Hit - 5 Sounds
Jump - 11 Sounds
Power-up - 11 Sounds
